IT-related Ethics

Guidelines for Developers

Developers play a crucial role in ensuring ethical practices in software development. Some key guidelines for developers include:

  • Respect user privacy by implementing robust data protection measures.
  • Avoid biased algorithms and ensure fairness in AI systems.
  • Transparently disclose any potential conflicts of interest.

Ethical Considerations for Data Scientists

Data scientists must consider various ethical principles in their work, including:

  • Obtaining informed consent when collecting and using data.
  • Protecting sensitive information through anonymization and encryption.
  • Avoiding bias in data analysis and decision-making processes.
